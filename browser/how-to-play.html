8-bit battle zone

Predict, outsmart, and out wit your opponent in this turn based strategy game!

How to Play

Attack

Damage your opponent by hitting them with an attack if your opponent doesn't shield then the attack hit causing between 15-19% damage.

Shield

Shields can be used to block against your opponents attack. If an attack is shielded your opponent will use focus but you won't take damage.

Focus

The green bar is your focus metre. In order to attack you need to have focus. If you attack it will use a portion of your focus, always be aware of how much focus you have as it is crucial in planning your victory.

SMASH attack

When your opponent is badly damaged (usually above 80%) then the smash attack icon will appear. The smash attack works in the same way as a normal attack except it deal a tremendous amount of damage and you can win the battle using it. Remember you need to have enough focus to use the smash attack, if you do not have enough focus then the smash attack icon will disappear. If a smash attack icon appears in your opponents stats area then it means your opponent has a smash attack available.

How to Win

There are two ways to win.

If you damage your opponent by 150% or more then you will win the game

If you use a smash attack and it hits your opponenent then you can win the game, but first you must beat them in a button bashing contest! You have 5 seconds to tap, click or bash more times than your opponent. If you lose your smash attack will still do damage but you won't automatically win the game.
